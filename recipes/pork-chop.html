Pork Chop

Ingredients

PREP TIME: 10 minutes

COOK TIME: 20 min

  • 1 Pork Chop
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • Garlic minced
  • Half stick butter seeded
  • 1 tsp rosemerry
  • 1 cup beef stock or broth

Instructions

  1. Mix Salt, Pepper, & Flour in a bowl
  2. Heat pan over Med-Heat with oil
  3. Coat Pork Chop in flour mixture
  4. Cook pork chop until golden brown on each side, (3 min each side)
  5. For Sauce:
  6. Add minced garlic, rosemerry, butter, stock/broth to a pan
  7. Simmer for 3 min
  8. Add juices from pork chop
  9. Wait until sauce thickens
  10. Serve pork chop with potatoes, green beans.
