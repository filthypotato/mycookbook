Instant Pot Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients

PREP TIME: 15 minutes

COOK TIME: 25 minutes

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 bell peppers,thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet of fajita seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken brothor chicken bouillon
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. Start by slicing the chicken breasts into thin strips. Thinly slice the bell peppers and onion as well. Mince the garlic cloves and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine the chicken taco seasoning, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Toss the chicken strips in the seasoning mixture until evenly coated.
  3. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté mode and heat the olive oil. Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and minced garlic. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  4. Push the sautéed veggies to the sides of the Instant Pot and add the seasoned chicken strips to the center. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the chicken is lightly browned on all sides.
  5. Pour the chicken broth and lime juice into the Instant Pot. Use a wooden spoon to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Cancel the sauté mode. Secure the Instant Pot lid and set the pressure release valve to the sealing position. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  6. Once the cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick pressure release. Carefully open the lid and give the contents a gentle stir. Your Instant Pot Chicken Fajitas are now ready to be served.
  7. Warm up the tortillas and spoon the chicken and veggie mixture onto each tortilla. Add your favorite optional toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped cilantro.
